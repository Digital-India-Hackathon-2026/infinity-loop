## Idea
Farmer2Gov is a digital platform that connects farmers directly with government procurement centers, eliminating middlemen who often reduce farmers' profits. Many farmers struggle with unfair pricing, delayed payments, and a complicated selling process.
Our platform allows farmers to register, upload crop details and photos, check real-time MSP (Minimum Support Price), and book procurement slots. Farmer2Gov analyzes crop images to provide an initial quality assessment, helping speed up verification. Government officials verify the produce and  ensure transparency and faster payments. The platform also supports local languages, voice assistance, making it easy for all farmers to use.
By removing intermediaries and digitizing procurement,Farmer2Gov ensures fair prices, improves farmers' income, reduces corruption, and creates a transparent, efficient, and farmer-friendly procurement system that supports the vision of Digital India.
